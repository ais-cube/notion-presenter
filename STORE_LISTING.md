## Short Description (132 chars max)
Turn published Notion pages into beautiful presentations. Split by headings or dividers, 4 themes, keyboard navigation.

## Detailed Description
Transform any published Notion page into a clean, full-screen presentation with one click.

FEATURES:
• 4 split modes — divide slides by horizontal dividers, H1, H2, or H1+H2 headings
• 4 visual themes — Light, Dark, Notion Classic, and Gradient Dark
• H1/H2 hierarchy — sub-heading slides show their parent heading for context
• Keyboard navigation — Arrow keys, Space, Home/End, Escape, F for fullscreen
• Touch support — swipe left/right on mobile devices
• Progress bar and slide counter
• Preserves Notion formatting: tables, code blocks, images, callouts, toggle blocks

HOW TO USE:
1. Open any published Notion page (*.notion.site)
2. Click the Notion Presenter icon in your toolbar
3. Choose split mode and theme
4. Click "Start Presentation"

PRIVACY:
• No data collection — everything runs locally in your browser
• No external servers or analytics
• Open source

Perfect for teachers, presenters, and anyone who writes content in Notion and wants to present it without switching tools.
